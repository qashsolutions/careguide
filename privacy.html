PRIVACY POLICY

How Your Data is Stored

All your health data is stored securely using Google Firebase services. Your phone number is encrypted (hashed) and used as an anonymous identifier. This allows you to:

  • Access your data from any device after phone verification
  • Share data with the member you invite
  • Keep your data private - we cannot see or access it

What Information We Store

We only store health information you choose to enter:

  • Medications, dosages, and schedules
  • Healthcare provider contacts
  • Appointment information
  • Health documents you upload
  • Audio memos you record

Firebase Services Used

We use these Google Firebase services to securely store and sync your data:

  • Firebase Authentication: Phone number verification using OTP
  • Firebase Firestore: Secure database storage
  • Firebase Storage: Voice Memo, Document and Image storage

All Firebase data is encrypted and tied to your hashed phone number, not your personal identity.

Device Permissions

Careguide requests these permissions to function:

Camera

  • Used to scan medical documents
  • Photos are uploaded to secure Firebase storage
  • Only accessible by you and invited group members

Photo Library

  • Used to save and retrieve medication images
  • Access controlled by iOS permissions

Microphone

  • Used for recording care memos
  • Audio stored securely in Firebase
  • Only you and group members can access

Contacts

  • Used to add healthcare providers to your contact list
  • Contact information stays in your device's contact app
  • No contact data sent to our servers

Notifications

  • Used for medication and appointment reminders
  • Scheduled locally on your device
  • You control all notification settings

Family Groups

  • Groups are created with 6-character invite codes
  • Maximum 2 members per group (1 admin + 1 member)
  • Group data shared securely through Firebase
  • Admin controls member access
  • Members can view but not edit group data

Data Security

  • Firebase Security: Google's enterprise-grade encryption
  • Phone Number Hashing: Your number is encrypted before storage
  • No Personal Identifiers: No names, emails, or addresses stored
  • Access Control: Only you can access your data via phone verification

Third-Party Services

We only use Google Firebase services:

  • ✅ Firebase Authentication, Firestore, Storage
  • ❌ No advertising networks
  • ❌ No social media integrations
  • ❌ No analytics or tracking services
  • ❌ No AI or machine learning services

Data Deletion

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete individual items anytime within the app
  • Leave groups to stop sharing data
  • Contact us to delete all your data permanently
  • Uninstalling the app stops new data sync but doesn't delete stored data

Children's Privacy

Careguide is designed for adults managing healthcare. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
