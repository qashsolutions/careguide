PRIVACY POLICY

How Your Data is Stored

100% Local Storage:

All data remains exclusively on your iPhone:

  • ✅ Medications and schedules - Stored in local database
  • ✅ Completely anonymized Healthcare contacts - Saved locally only
  • ✅ Completely anonymized Audio memos - Recorded and kept on device
  • ✅ Completely anonymized Document scans - Stored in app's local storage
  • ✅ Completely anonymized Group codes - Generated locally, expire in 24 hours

Apple CareKit Framework

CareGuide uses Apple's CareKit framework for medication scheduling, care plan management, and health tracking features. All CareKit data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers or shared with third parties.

Device Permissions

CareGuide requests certain permissions to function. Here's exactly how we use them:

Camera

  • Used ONLY to scan medical documents
  • Photos are saved locally in your device's app storage
  • Never uploaded to any server
  • You control when camera is accessed

Microphone

  • Used ONLY for recording care memos
  • Limited to 60-second recordings
  • Maximum 10 memos stored locally
  • Audio never leaves your device

Face ID/Touch ID

  • Used for secure app access
  • Biometric data stays in iPhone's Secure Enclave
  • We cannot access your biometric information
  • Optional - you can use passcode instead

Notifications

  • Used for medication reminders only
  • All notifications are scheduled locally
  • No push notifications from servers
  • You control notification settings

Data Security

  • Encryption: iOS automatically encrypts app data when device is locked
  • Face ID Protection: App requires authentication to access
  • No Network Transmission: Health data never sent over internet
  • Local Only: All processing happens on your device

Family Groups - Optional Feature

  • Groups are created with 6-character codes
  • Codes expire after 24 hours for security
  • Maximum 3 members per group
  • No data is shared between devices
  • Group codes are for invitation only

Third-Party Services

We use NO third-party services:

  • ❌ No analytics (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • ❌ No crash reporting services
  • ❌ No advertising networks
  • ❌ No social media integrations
  • ❌ No AI or machine learning services
  • ❌ No cloud storage providers

Data Deletion

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete individual items anytime within the app
  • Uninstalling the app removes ALL data permanently
  • No data remains after app deletion
  • We cannot recover deleted data (we never had it)

Children's Privacy

CareGuide is designed for adult caregivers managing healthcare. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app requires Face ID/Touch ID authentication typically available to device owners.
